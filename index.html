Research Team

Prof. Lin has been the dean of the Artificial Intelligence Research Institute of Zhejiang Laboratory since 2021. Before that, Prof Lin was the Director of Bioinfomatics Research Centre at School of Computer Engineering, Nanyang Technological University.

Prof Lin's team invented a high-order fiber mode for manipulating single cells which can fundamentally solve the bottleneck problems of cell obstacles in training sample labeling. Granted the National Lab Strategic Programme on Humane-like Intelligence, Prof Lin‚Äôs team developed the Model-as-a-Service based AI open-source platform which has greatly promoted the development of the AI apps, under the leadership of Prof. Pan Yunhe, academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, addressing artificial intelligence that goes beyond data and knowledge-driven. Approved by Zhejiang Province Development and Reform Commission, his team set up the Zhejiang Province Engineering Center of Digital Rehabilitation Equipment, and has achieved scientific breakthroughs in multimodal and domain-oriented large-scale pre-trained models, alignment of multimodal sensors, quantitative representation of domain knowledge, establishment of physiological models, human digital twin and edge-computing for embodied intelligence robots, with four granted patents and more submitted ones.
